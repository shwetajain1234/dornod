Instructions to play
    • 1. Choose your initial box. This box will be your box for the game.
      2. You can then choose to open and eliminate 6 boxes.
      3. The banker will then call you with an offer to buy your box
      4. You can then choose to accept his offer by clicking on the Deal button, or you can reject him by clicking on the No Deal button.
      5. If you clicked on the No Deal Button, the game continues.
      6. At the end of the game, you have the option to switch boxes when there is 1 box left.
      7. Open your box to find out how much money there is inside.
